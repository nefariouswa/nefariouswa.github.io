NEFARIOUS:  EVENT RULES

By your entry into NEFARIOUS event, you acknowledge that you are over the age of 18 years, have read these rules, understand them, and will abide by them, and that you assume full responsibility for all risks during your attendance at this venue.

At least 1 (one) Dungeon Monitor (DM) shall be present during the event.

There shall be 1 (one) first - aid officer on site at all times during the event.

Please remember we have a lot of new kinksters attending each event. Be patient and respectful with them – we were all new once.

The house caution word is AMBER. The house safe word is RED to immediately stop play.   The house outside assistance required word is MAYDAY.

A Dungeon Monitor (DM) may, and will, intervene in a scene if s/he hears the word RED, and the scene is not interrupted immediately by the participants.

DM’s must be obeyed for your safety, and the safety of all patrons and crew.

Please notify the DM or NEFARIOUS crew of any safety breach, harassment or etiquette infringement problems that come to you\r attention.

The DM and NEFARIOUS crew reserves the right to declare any toy, implement, object or practice as unsafe for any reason, and require that it be removed from the facility immediately.

No cameras, video or audio recording devices are allowed in the venue.  There shall be a designated photographer for each event at NEFARIOUS.

Leave all equipment free of bodily fluids, wax, toys; etc... there shall be
cleaning supplies at the venue to facilitate this. Please bring any fluid contamination found at the venue, to the attention of NEFARIOUS crew.

Normal party etiquette is expected. Do not interfere in a scene. Do not touch anyone or anyone's gear without permission. Watching is acceptable from a safe distance. Please keep all conversation, laughter and comments to a minimum in the play space. Play fair. Please do not monopolise equipment to the extent that others may not have the time to play. Abnormally loud screaming and talking within a scene is discouraged as a courtesy to others.

No illegal substances of any kind may be brought into the venue. Any patron found by the DM or NEFARIOUS crew to be under the influence of drugs or excessive alcohol will be asked to leave the venue immediately. Liquor icensing Act 1988 and revision shall be in force during the event.

Venue rules do not permit full nudity. Minimum dress requirement are a bra that covers the nipple and bikini briefs. Footwear is to be worn at all times unless in suspension areas.

Smoking is only permitted in our outdoor designated smoking area.

The DM must be informed of any advanced edge play prior to the beginning of the event. This includes knife/needle play , breath play and electro play.

The Drawbridge program attendees will be permitted into the venue at 6pm. The NEFARIOUS event will open at 7pm precisely and absolutely no admittance will be granted after 10pm. Exit from the venue is only by the front door.

All guests are responsible for any equipment, clothing and all personal items that are brought into the venue. Please be aware and alert at all times.

No member of NEFARIOUS crew or operators of the facility, nor any agent, successors or assigns of any of the foregoing shall be held liable to any attendee for injury to a person incurred as a result of attendance at this event.

NEFARIOUS is a safe, sane consensual and RISK AWARE

CONSENSUAL KINK Perth alternate community event.

SO PLAY NICE AND ENJOY WHAT YOU CAN CREATE.
