NEFARIOUS:  DRAWBRIDGE FAQ's:

All your questions answered.

What is the Nefarious' Drawbridge program all about?

The Drawbridge program aims to promote familiarity and comfort within the Nefarious events and community. This is done through the Drawbridge Hour, and having ongoing support available during each event within the Drawbridge Space.

Who runs the Drawbridge?

The Nefarious Drawbridge Team. They run the Hour, and are the main supports in the Space - though any of the Nefarious Crew may help Drawbridge attendees throughout the event.

What is the Drawbridge Hour?

The Hour ‪is a one hour program ‬run before each Nefarious event‪ which orients and informs attendees‬ in a calm and well-lit space. It is a time for exploration of the venue, and discussion led by the Drawbridge Team.

When is the Drawbridge Hour?

The Hour runs before each Nefarious event. It commences at 6pm - the door will open briefly just before this for tickets and waiver signing.

Who can come to the Hour?

The Hour is open to anyone who wishes to attend - though it is aimed at those newer to the community or fetish events. Entry to the Hour is included with each Nefarious ticket.

What happens in the Drawbridge Space?

The Space is available to any attendees needing assistance, or even someone to talk to. Just have a seat, and a member of the Drawbridge Team (or Nefarious Crew) will come and see you.
What else do I need to know?

You do not have to attend the Hour to make use of the Drawbridge space throughout the event. RSVPs to the Hour are appreciated, but not required.

Want to know more?

If you have any questions about the Drawbridge, you can contact the Drawbridge Team at draw_bridge@hotmail.com
